Greed City

Get ready for an exhilarating adventure designed for 4-8 players, where fortunes rise like the tallest buildings in the skyline! Let fate decide your starting point with a simple coin toss. The player who rolls the highest number on the die, will select their character first, and so on, launching us into an epic pursuit of wealth. In Greed City, success is inevitable!
